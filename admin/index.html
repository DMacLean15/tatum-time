🏀 Writing a Great Tatum Time Post

Everything's optional — plain writing already looks great. Use the preview on the right to see how it'll look.

⭐ Make it pop

  • Give it a punchy headline — it's the first thing readers see.
  • Keep paragraphs short. Break up long posts with headings.
  • Add a photo or YouTube clip — posts with visuals get read more.
  • Sprinkle in green stat tags for the key numbers.

📋 Post starters (optional structures to riff on)

🏀 Game recap: Final score → 1–2 standout performers (with stat tags) → the turning point → what's next.

📰 Trade / news reaction: What happened → why it matters for the C's → your grade or hot take.

⭐ Player spotlight: Quick intro → the numbers that pop → why he matters to the team.

🟢 Green stat tags

Wrap any stat in == to make a green highlight:

Tatum went off for ==28.3 PPG== → 28.3 PPG on the live site.

Heads up: in the preview it shows as plain ==28.3 PPG==, but it turns green on the published site.

✍️ Formatting toolbar

Use the toolbar above the writing box for bold, italics, Headings, bullet/numbered lists, links, and quotes.

📸 Photos

  • Click the image button and upload — it auto-resizes to fit.
  • The little thumbnail sometimes doesn't show while attaching — that's cosmetic. Check the preview to confirm the photo's there.
  • Shrink huge phone photos before uploading so the site stays fast.

▶️ YouTube videos

Two ways: paste a link on its own line, OR click the "+" in the editor → "YouTube Video" and paste the link.

🏀 Box score card

Click the "+" in the editor → "Box Score", type the two teams and scores, and it drops in a clean final-score card (winner shown in green).

🏀 "See full game stats" button

Fill in the Game Box-Score Link field with an NBA.com box-score web address, and a green button appears on the post. Leave it blank for none.

🍀 Banner emoji & publishing

  • The Banner Emoji you pick shows on the post's banner card.
  • Drag your newest post to the TOP of the list.
  • Get it looking right in the preview, then hit Publish once. Edit as much as you want — it's free and unlimited.
