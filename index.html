About

A blog dedicated to the Boston Celtics

Tatum Time is built for serious Celtics fans. It's a place for honest analysis, statistical trends, and everything happening around the green and white.

Dan brings a sharp eye for the numbers — years deep into fantasy basketball on Fantrax, he's obsessed with tracking the trends that the box score doesn't always show, from per-40 projections to under-the-radar bench breakouts.

If it has anything to do with Boston basketball, you'll find it here. Welcome to Tatum Time.
