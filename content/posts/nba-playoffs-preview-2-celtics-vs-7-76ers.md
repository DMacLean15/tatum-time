Joel Embiid is ruled out for Game 1 with the recurring injury concerns that have shadowed his season, leaving the Sixers leaning on backups Adem Bona and Andre Drummond up front. The offense will run through:

- **Tyrese Maxey** — ==28.3 PPG== the engine of the attack.

- **V.J. Edgecombe** — promising rookie at ==16 PPG== ==5.6 RPG== ==4.2 APG==.

#### Boston's Edge

It starts with **The Jays** — Jayson Tatum and Jaylen Brown — backed by sixth man Payton Pritchard and a deep frontcourt of Neemias Queta, Nikola Vucevic and Luka Garza. With Philadelphia undermanned in the paint, Boston's depth advantage looms large. Advantage green.
