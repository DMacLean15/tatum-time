April 10, 2026 was a downpour. The Celtics buried **29 three-pointers** — tying the NBA single-game record — in a ==144–118== demolition of the Pelicans, their 10th straight win.

#### Make It Rain ☔

Sam Hauser led the barrage with **eight threes**. The distribution was absurd — eight different Celtics hit two or more from deep:

- Hauser (8) · Pritchard (5) · Vucevic & White (3) · Brown, Walsh, González & Scheierman (2)
- And the cherry on top: **Neemias Queta drained the first three-pointer of his career.**

#### Jaylen Brown's Big Year

Brown is putting together a career campaign — ==28.8 PPG== (4th in the NBA), ==7 RPG==, ==5.2 APG==. Bona-fide All-Star, and essential to everything Boston does.

#### Tatum Heating Up

Back from injury, JT looks the part. Over his last ten: **22.4 PPG, 10.9 RPG, 6.1 APG**, including a triple-double. With the fourth-best record in the league behind only the Thunder, Spurs and Pistons, Boston is rolling toward the postseason.
