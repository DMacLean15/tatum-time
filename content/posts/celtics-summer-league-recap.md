The Celtics kicked off their Summer League Debut with a thrilling OT 83-80 victory over the Toronto Raptors on Friday July 10th.

![](/images/uploads/img_1885.jpg)

The Player Of The Game was returning Big Man Amari Williams who went off for 23 Points, 13 Rebounds and 2 Blocks.

![](/images/uploads/img_1886.jpg)

The Celtics secured their second consecutive Summer League victory with a 87-72 win over The Charlotte Hornets on Sunday July 12th.

![](/images/uploads/img_1887.jpg)

The Player Of The Game was Celtics Second Round Draft Pick Dillon Mitchell who exploded for 24 Points, 8 Rebounds and 6 Steals.

![](/images/uploads/img_1888.jpg)

HIGHLIGHT: Maine Celtics Guard John Tonje was scorching hot across the first two Vegas Summer League games putting up 37 Points on 12/24 FG and a bonkers 8-13 3PM.

![](/images/uploads/img_1883.jpg)

The Celtics fell to the Hawks 102-90 in Game 3 of the Summer League action. It was a well-rounded effort with no real top contributor as the Hawks secured the victory on Monday July 13th.

![](/images/uploads/img_1890.jpg)

The Celtics bounced back nicely from their defeat against the Hawks to the tune of an 82-76 victory over the Sacramento Kings on Wednesday July 15th.

![](/images/uploads/img_1891.jpg)

The Player Of The Game was Celtics explosive Guard from Spain Hugo Gonzalez who went off across the board with 24 Points, 10 Rebounds and 5 Assists.

![](/images/uploads/img_1881.jpg)

The Celtics wrapped up their Summer League in a disappointing fashion falling to the Magic 103-97 in the Consolation Round on Saturday July 18th.

Concluding Thoughts: There were a plethora of standout performances as the Celtics finished with an overall 3-2 record. They came out of the gates guns blazing, starting out hot at 2-0. The Hawks got the best of them in Game 3 and unfortunately, that lone loss sent them to the Consolation Round, where they would ultimately fall to the Magic.

Big Dan’s Celtics Summer League MVP:

John Tonje 🔥🦞

![](/images/uploads/img_1894.jpg)

He finished with: 74 Points, 17 Rebounds, 5 Assists, 6 Steals and 14 3PM across 5 games. Very well-deserved 🏀🏆😎
