The Boston Celtics kick off the Las Vegas Summer league on July 10th. They are scheduled to play in 4 preliminary games:

Friday July 10 vs. Toronto Raptors @ 9 PM ET (ESPN)

Sunday July 12 vs. Charlotte Hornets @ 5 PM ET (ESPN 2)

Monday July 13 vs. Atlanta Hawks @ 6 PM ET (Prime Video)

Wednesday July 15 vs. Sacramento Kings @ 8 PM ET (ESPN 2)

TBD: A 5th game will be scheduled after July 15th depending on the preliminary standings, followed by potential playoff matchups.

Key players include recent draft picks Chris Cenac Jr and Dillion Mitchell:
